## Reading Goals

### Types of Goals

BookShelf supports **6 different challenge types**:

| Goal Type | Description | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| **Total Books** | Read X books this year | 52 books in 2024 |
| **Genre Challenge** | Read X books in a genre | 12 Fantasy books |
| **Author Challenge** | Read X books by an author | 5 Brandon Sanderson books |
| **Format Goal** | Read X books in a format | 10 audiobooks |
| **Page Count** | Read X total pages | 20,000 pages |
| **Monthly Target** | Read X books per month | 4 books monthly |

### Creating a Goal

1. Navigate to **Stats â†’ Goals**
2. Click **+ New Goal**

![Create Goal Modal](../images/create-goal-modal.png)
*Set your reading challenge*

3. Choose goal type
4. Set your target
5. Select the time period
6. Click **Create**

### Goal Examples

**The Classic Challenge:**
- Type: Total Books
- Target: 52
- Period: Year 2024
- *One book a week!*

**Genre Exploration:**
- Type: Genre Challenge
- Genre: Science Fiction
- Target: 12
- *Discover new worlds*

**Author Deep Dive:**
- Type: Author Challenge
- Author: Agatha Christie
- Target: 10

## Reading Streaks

Maintain your momentum!

![Reading Streak](../images/reading-streak.png)
*Don't break the chain!*

### How Streaks Work

A streak day is counted when you:
- Log any reading progress, OR
- Finish a book, OR
- Add reading time

### Streak Stats

| Stat | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Current Streak** | Consecutive days (so far) |
| **Longest Streak** | Your personal best |
| **Total Reading Days** | All-time count |

### Keeping the Streak

Even busy days count! Just:
- Read a few pages
- Log some progress in the app
- Finish that chapter you started

## Tracking Reading Dates

### Setting Read Dates

For accurate stats, track when you read:

![Read Dates](../images/read-dates-input.png)
*When did you read it?*

**For each book:**
- **Start Date** â€” When you began
- **End Date** â€” When you finished

### Bulk Date Entry

Importing old reading history? Use bulk edit:

1. Select books
2. Choose **Set Dates**
3. Enter the year (or specific dates)

### Retroactive Tracking

Adding books from years past?

1. Set status to "Read"
2. Add the approximate date finished
3. Stats will recalculate automatically

## Stats Tips & Tricks

### For Best Accuracy

- Always set **Start Date** when you begin a book
- Mark **End Date** when you finish
- Log page counts for meaningful stats

### Fixing Past Data

Forgot to track a book?

1. Open the book
2. Edit â†’ Reading tab

## Goal Strategies

### The One-a-Week Plan

- 52 books per year
- Mix short and long books
- Include novellas for buffer

### Genre Rotation

- Set goals for multiple genres
- Discover new favorites
- Break out of comfort zones

### The Page Count Method

Instead of book count:
- Set 15,000 pages/year
- Allows for long epics
- No pressure to pick short books

### Monthly Sprints

- 4-5 books per month goals
- Adjust by season
- Summer reading challenges!
