## Quick Look: What Can BookShelf Do?

### Your Personal Library, Supercharged

![Dashboard Overview](../images/dashboard-overview.png)
*Your dashboard â€” where your reading journey begins each day*

- **Track Everything**: From that dog-eared paperback to your latest Audible addiction
- **Multiple Formats**: Physical books, ebooks, audiobooks â€” we don't discriminate
- **Reading Status**: To-Read, Currently Reading, Finished, DNF (we don't judge!)
- **Beautiful Stats**: Graphs, charts, and a GitHub-style reading heatmap

### Read Right in the App

![Ebook Reader](../images/ebook-reader-preview.png)
*The built-in reader â€” no app-switching required*

- **EPUB Support**: Full-featured with themes, fonts, and bookmarks
- **PDF Viewing**: For those fancy illustrated editions
- **Comic/Manga Reader (CBZ)**: Panel-by-panel bliss for graphic novel fans

### Metadata Magic

Stop typing! BookShelf fetches book info from **6 different sources**:
- Google Books
- Open Library
- Goodreads
- Hardcover
- Amazon
- Comic Vine

### Smart Organization

- **Series Tracking**: Including those pesky .5 novellas
- **Multi-Author Support**: Because collaborations exist
- **Tags & Genres**: Your taxonomy, your rules
- **Magic Shelves**: Auto-updating smart collections

## The Story So Far...

BookShelf V2 is a complete rewrite of the original BookShelf. Why? Because:

1. **Modern Stack**: Built with SvelteKit, Tailwind CSS, and all the good stuff
2. **Better Performance**: Snappy, responsive, and a joy to use
3. **More Features**: Years of requested features, finally implemented
4. **Self-Hosted Freedom**: Your data, your server, your rules
