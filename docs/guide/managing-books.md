## Adding Books

### Metadata Search (Recommended)

1. Click **+ Add Book**
2. Type the book title, author, or ISBN
3. Select from results â€” BookShelf searches **6 sources**:
   - Google Books
   - Open Library
   - Goodreads
   - Hardcover
   - Amazon
   - Comic Vine
4. Click **Add to Library**

### Manual Entry

For rare books not in any database:

1. Click **+ Add Book** â†’ **Manual** tab
2. Fill in details (only title is required!)
3. Upload a cover image if you have one
4. Click **Add Book**

## Authors & Series

### Multiple Authors

Books can have multiple authors with roles:
- Author, Co-Author
- Editor, Translator
- Illustrator, Narrator

### Series Tracking

- Assign books to series with position numbers
- Supports decimals (2.5 for novellas!)
- Books can be in multiple series
